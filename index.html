Field of Experience

The Field of Experience is a living framework that maps the dynamic movements of human experience. Each section introduces a dimension of the field and links to a dedicated chapter.

Sections

  • Curiosity — The initiating movement of the field, where perception opens and exploration begins.
  • Memory — The field's retention layer, shaping patterns, expectations, and recurring influences.
  • Projections — The outward movement of assumptions, anticipations, and imagined futures.
  • Cycle — The rhythmic patterning of experience: repetition, reinforcement, and renewal.
  • Inquiry — The clarifying movement that reveals structure, dissolves distortion, and sharpens perception.
  • Synthesis — The integrative movement where disparate elements resolve into coherence.
  • The Now — The immediate field in which all movements converge and become lived experience.
  • Conclusion — A reflection on the total field and its ongoing evolution.
  • Comparative Cosmology — A harmonic exploration of how cosmologies arise from oscillations within the experiential field.
  • Universal Principles of Existence — Foundational patterns that describe the deep structural movements underlying all experiential processes.
